# MusicStudioLab Enterprise DAW 6.9

## Draggable rotary controls

All shared rotary controls now behave as direct-manipulation DAW knobs rather than hidden range inputs only.

- Press and drag upward or right to increase a value.
- Drag downward or left to decrease it.
- Pointer capture keeps adjustment active outside the knob face until release.
- Hold **Shift** for fine adjustment.
- Hold **Alt/Option** for ultra-fine adjustment.
- The knob face displays focused and active-drag states.
- Controls retain their configured minimum, maximum, and step values.
- Focused controls support Arrow keys, Page Up/Page Down, Home, and End.
- Accessible slider values and formatted value text are exposed to assistive technology.

The behavior applies throughout Mixer, Synth Lab, oscillator and layer controls, filters, envelopes, LFOs, sample editing, effects, and Mastering.

## Master-volume slider

A horizontal master output control is now permanently available in the top transport.

- Output range: **0% to 125%**.
- Live percentage readout.
- Filled level visualization and draggable slider handle.
- Persistent in project autosave and portable project files.
- Updates the active Web Audio master gain without restarting playback.
- Controls sample and synthesizer previews.
- Applies to the exported offline master WAV.
- Remains synchronized with the existing Master knob in the Mastering workspace.
- Existing limiter-ceiling gain staging remains active after the selected master level.

## Retained production features

- 444 original local WAV assets.
- 240 original layered synthesizer presets across 20 categories.
- Direct synthesizer-track creation and bulk track management.
- Selective mute, mute all, delete selected, and delete all.
- Full C0-C9 piano roll with scale guides and FL-style ghost notes.
- Note edge resizing, center movement, marquee selection, group transforms, fills, and 1-16 bar patterns.
- Realtime Web Audio scheduling and offline stereo WAV rendering.
- Vite client/SSR builds, route prerendering, and SEO validation.
